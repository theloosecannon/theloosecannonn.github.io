Frequently asked questions

  1. How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?

    2. At http://print.hz.nl you can log in with your HZ account and upload a file that you want to print, using your HZ pass by any HZ printers you will be able print them.

  2. How can you scan a document a send it to your laptop at HZ?

    3. At a HZ printer you can scan your pas and choose scan and the files will be send to your mail box.

  3. Where can I find the latest instructions on what to do when you are sick / show symptoms of COVID?

    4. Contact the teachers/helpdesk at school and stay home. Follow the lessons on stream to compensate for that unusual sickness.

  4. How can you book a project space in one of the wings?

    5. On the home page on Self Service Portal you can choose Reservations & visitors and you will be able to book a room

  5. What are the instructions if you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot?

    6. Activate your HZ pass at first floor and when you want to park there you can scan your HZ pass.
